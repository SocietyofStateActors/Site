Imperare sibi maximum imperium est

Our organ she sings a tune only heard,
By those who can read the Language of Birds.
When summer is near, we seek out their light,
And carry them greedily into the night.

The Twelve that are cast from our upperclass,
Burst into flames and turn into ash,
Rising from dust and thanking the heavens,
They search for the treasure that's buried in Evans.
